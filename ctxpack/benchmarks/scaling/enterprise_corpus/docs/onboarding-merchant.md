# Merchant Onboarding Guide

## Overview

This document outlines the complete merchant onboarding process for the ShopStream marketplace. The onboarding flow takes a merchant from initial application through verification, setup, and first product listing. The target time from application to first live product is 5 business days for standard merchants and 2 business days for enterprise merchants with pre-negotiated agreements.

## Onboarding Stages

### Stage 1: Application Submission

Merchants apply through the ShopStream Partner Portal (partners.shopstream.com) or through a referral from the business development team. The application collects:

1. **Business Information**
   - Legal business name (must match tax documents)
   - Business type (Sole Proprietor, LLC, Corporation, Partnership, Non-Profit)
   - Country of registration
   - Tax identification number (EIN for US, VAT for EU)
   - Year established
   - Expected monthly order volume

2. **Contact Information**
   - Primary contact name and email
   - Business phone number
   - Business address

3. **Product Information**
   - Primary product categories
   - Estimated number of SKUs
   - Average price range
   - Whether products require age verification
   - Whether products are regulated (food, supplements, etc.)

4. **Fulfillment Details**
   - Warehouse locations (at least one required)
   - Average fulfillment time
   - Shipping carriers used
   - Whether interested in ShopStream Fulfillment Network (SFN)

**System Actions:**
- A Merchant record is created with status PENDING_VERIFICATION and verification_status UNVERIFIED
- A unique merchant_id (UUID v4) is generated by the merchant-service
- An account manager is assigned based on the merchant's region and expected volume
- A welcome email is sent with next steps and document upload instructions

### Stage 2: Document Verification

Required documents vary by country and business type:

**United States:**
- Business registration certificate (LLC Articles, Corp Certificate of Incorporation)
- EIN confirmation letter (IRS Form CP 575 or SS-4)
- Government-issued photo ID of the primary business owner
- Voided check or bank statement for settlement account verification

**European Union:**
- Business registration extract (e.g., Handelsregister, Registre du Commerce)
- VAT registration certificate
- Proof of identity (passport or national ID) for authorized representative
- Bank account verification (IBAN confirmation)

**United Kingdom:**
- Companies House registration certificate
- VAT certificate (if VAT-registered)
- Director's photo ID
- Bank account verification

**Document Processing:**
- Documents are uploaded through the Partner Portal's secure document center
- Automated OCR extracts key fields (business name, tax ID, address) and cross-references with the application
- Manual verification by the trust & safety team within 2 business days
- If documents are incomplete or unclear, the merchant is contacted for clarification
- Verification_status transitions: UNVERIFIED → DOCUMENTS_SUBMITTED → VERIFIED (or VERIFICATION_FAILED)

### Stage 3: Agreement and Commission Setup

Once verified, the merchant enters the agreement phase:

1. **Service Agreement**
   - Standard Terms of Service (ToS) for marketplace participation
   - Data Processing Agreement (DPA) for customer data handling
   - Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) for product listings and merchant conduct

2. **Commission Structure**
   - Default rate: 15% of order subtotal (before tax and shipping)
   - Volume-based tiers available for merchants with monthly GMV above $50,000:
     - $0 - $49,999 GMV: 15% commission
     - $50,000 - $199,999 GMV: 12% commission
     - $200,000+ GMV: 10% commission
   - Enterprise merchants may negotiate custom rates
   - Commission is stored in the Merchant entity's commission_rate and commission_tiers fields

3. **Settlement Configuration**
   - Settlement schedule: Daily, Weekly, Biweekly (default), or Monthly
   - Bank account registration for ACH/SEPA/wire transfers
   - Bank account verified via micro-deposit confirmation (2-3 business days)
   - Settlement currency set based on merchant's country

4. **Fee Schedule**
   - Payment processing: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction (Stripe standard rate)
   - Fraud protection: included at no additional cost (Sift Science integration)
   - SFN fulfillment: $3.50 per order + $0.50/lb after 1 lb (optional)
   - SFN storage: $0.75/cubic foot/month (optional)
   - Chargeback fee: $15.00 per chargeback
   - Currency conversion: 1.5% markup on mid-market exchange rate

### Stage 4: Store Setup

After the agreement is signed, the merchant sets up their storefront:

1. **Store Creation**
   - At least one MerchantStore must be created
   - Store name, type (Physical, Virtual, Hybrid), and contact information
   - Physical stores require full address and operating hours
   - Virtual stores are automatically created for online-only merchants

2. **Warehouse Registration**
   - At least one Warehouse must be registered
   - Address, timezone, shipping cutoff time
   - For SFN merchants, ShopStream-operated warehouses are assigned
   - Warehouse priority is set for fulfillment optimization

3. **Branding**
   - Store logo upload (minimum 400x400px, PNG or SVG)
   - Brand colors and banner image
   - Return policy (must meet platform minimum: 14-day return window)
   - Customer-facing store description

4. **Shipping Configuration**
   - Shipping zones and rates
   - Free shipping thresholds (optional)
   - Carrier integrations (USPS, UPS, FedEx, DHL)
   - Estimated delivery time configuration

### Stage 5: API and Integration Setup

Merchants who need programmatic access set up API credentials:

1. **API Key Generation**
   - Sandbox key (sk_test_XXX) created automatically for testing
   - Production key (sk_live_XXX) created after store setup is complete
   - Each key has configurable scopes (catalog:read, catalog:write, orders:read, etc.)
   - Rate limits based on merchant tier: Standard (100 req/min), Premium (500), Enterprise (2000)

2. **Webhook Configuration**
   - Merchants register webhook endpoints (must be HTTPS)
   - Available event types: order.created, order.updated, order.cancelled, payment.captured, payment.failed, shipment.created, shipment.delivered, inventory.low_stock, refund.completed
   - Webhooks include HMAC-SHA256 signature for payload verification
   - Retry policy: 8 attempts over 48 hours with exponential backoff

3. **ERP/WMS Integration**
   - Integration guides available for common platforms (SAP, NetSuite, ShipStation)
   - Inventory sync via API (push) or webhook (pull)
   - Order export in CSV, JSON, or XML formats

### Stage 6: Product Listing

The final step is listing products:

1. **Catalog Upload**
   - Single product creation via Merchant Portal
   - Bulk upload via CSV template (up to 10,000 products per upload)
   - API-based catalog sync for large catalogs

2. **Product Requirements**
   - Title: max 500 characters, no HTML, no promotional keywords
   - Description: max 10,000 characters, limited Markdown
   - At least 3 images recommended (minimum 800x800px)
   - At least one active ProductVariant per product
   - Products must be assigned to at least one leaf Category
   - Price must be greater than $0.01

3. **Product Review**
   - First-time merchants: first 5 products are manually reviewed by the catalog team (24-48 hours)
   - Subsequent products: automated quality checks only
   - Products flagged by automated checks are queued for manual review
   - Products in regulated categories (alcohol, supplements, weapons) always require manual review

4. **Going Live**
   - Merchant status transitions from PENDING_VERIFICATION to ACTIVE
   - Products status transitions from DRAFT to ACTIVE
   - Products are indexed in the search engine within 15 minutes
   - A "Welcome to ShopStream" notification is sent to the merchant's configured email

## Post-Onboarding

### First 30 Days
- Account manager conducts a check-in call at day 7 and day 30
- Merchant analytics dashboard becomes populated after first order
- Automated performance alerts are configured (fulfillment time, return rate, customer rating)

### Performance Standards
Merchants must maintain the following standards to remain in good standing:
- **Order fulfillment**: Ship within the committed timeframe (default: 3 business days)
- **Fulfillment rate**: >=95% of orders fulfilled without cancellation
- **Return rate**: <10% of orders returned (rolling 90 days)
- **Customer rating**: >=3.5 stars average (rolling 90 days)
- **Response time**: Customer messages answered within 24 hours

Merchants falling below these standards receive:
1. First violation: Warning email from account manager
2. Second violation (within 90 days): Status changed to UNDER_REVIEW
3. Third violation: Status changed to SUSPENDED, new orders paused
4. Continued failure: TERMINATED after appeal period

### Suspension Process
When a merchant is suspended:
- Merchant status changes to SUSPENDED
- No new orders can be placed with this merchant
- Existing orders in CONFIRMED or PROCESSING status continue to be fulfilled
- Products remain visible but show "Currently Unavailable" instead of "Add to Cart"
- The merchant's products' inventory is not decremented for new orders
- API keys remain active (for fulfilling existing orders) but catalog write operations are blocked
- The merchant receives a suspension notification with the reason and appeal process
- Suspension is logged in AuditLog with severity CRITICAL

## Common Issues During Onboarding

### Document Rejection Reasons
1. **Expired documents** — Registration certificates and IDs must be current
2. **Name mismatch** — Business name on documents must match the application exactly
3. **Illegible uploads** — Documents must be clear scans, not photos of screens
4. **Missing pages** — Multi-page documents must include all pages
5. **Wrong document type** — Must match the country-specific requirements listed above

### Bank Account Verification Issues
1. **Micro-deposit not received** — Allow 2-3 business days; check spam folder for notification
2. **Wrong amounts entered** — Three failed attempts locks the verification; contact support
3. **International accounts** — SWIFT/BIC code required for wire transfers outside US and EU

### Product Listing Issues
1. **Image too small** — Minimum 800x800px required
2. **Category not found** — Products must be in leaf categories; browse the category tree or contact catalog team
3. **Price validation** — Prices must be in the merchant's base currency; multi-currency display is automatic
