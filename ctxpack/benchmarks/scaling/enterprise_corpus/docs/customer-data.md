# Customer Data Management

## Overview

Customer data management on ShopStream encompasses identity resolution, data matching, privacy compliance, profile management, and analytics. This document covers the systems and processes that handle the customer lifecycle from registration through account deletion, with emphasis on data quality, deduplication, and privacy.

## Customer Identity

### Registration
Customers register on ShopStream through one of three methods:
1. **Email registration**: Customer provides email, name, and password. Email verification required before account activation.
2. **Social login**: OAuth 2.0 authentication via Google, Apple, or Facebook. Email is pre-verified by the social provider.
3. **Guest checkout to account**: After a guest checkout, the customer is offered to create an account using the order email.

All registration methods assign the same type of identifier:
- **customer_id**: UUID v4, generated by the identity-service, immutable
- **email**: Must be unique among active (non-deleted) customers
- **account_type**: INDIVIDUAL (default), BUSINESS, or ENTERPRISE

### Customer Matching and Deduplication

Duplicate customer accounts can arise from:
- Same person registering with different email addresses
- Guest checkouts creating order records without an account
- Social login creating a separate account from an existing email registration

**Deduplication Strategy:**
1. **Email-based matching**: Primary key for dedup. Same email = same customer.
2. **Phone-based matching**: Secondary signal. Same phone number across accounts flags potential duplicate.
3. **Address-based fuzzy matching**: Tertiary signal. Same address (normalized) with similar name triggers manual review.
4. **Device fingerprint matching**: If two accounts frequently use the same device fingerprint, they are flagged for potential merge.

**Merge Process:**
- Automated merges only happen for email-exact matches (e.g., guest checkout email matches registered account)
- All other merges require manual review by customer service
- When accounts are merged:
  - The older account is retained as the primary
  - Orders, reviews, and subscription from the secondary are re-linked to the primary
  - Addresses and payment methods are combined (duplicates removed)
  - Session and notification history from the secondary is retained but linked to primary
  - The secondary account is soft-deleted with a merge reference

## Customer Profile Data

### Core Fields
The Customer entity maintains the following profile data:
- **Identification**: customer_id (UUID), email, phone_number
- **Personal**: first_name, last_name, date_of_birth
- **Preferences**: preferred_language, preferred_currency
- **Loyalty**: loyalty_tier (BRONZE/SILVER/GOLD/PLATINUM), loyalty_points
- **Status**: status (PENDING_VERIFICATION/ACTIVE/SUSPENDED/DEACTIVATED/DELETED)
- **Consent**: marketing_consent (explicit opt-in required)
- **Timestamps**: created_at, updated_at, deleted_at

### Loyalty Program
The ShopStream loyalty program has four tiers based on trailing 12-month spend:
- **Bronze**: $0 - $499 spend. Default tier. No additional benefits.
- **Silver**: $500 - $1,999 spend. 5% bonus loyalty points.
- **Gold**: $2,000 - $4,999 spend. 10% bonus points + free standard shipping.
- **Platinum**: $5,000+ spend. 15% bonus points + free express shipping + early sale access.

Loyalty points mechanics:
- Earned at 1 point per $1 spent (before tax)
- Points expire 24 months after earning
- Redeemable at 100 points = $1 discount
- Points are non-transferable
- Tier recalculation runs monthly on the 1st

### Profile Updates
- Name, phone, and preferences can be updated by the customer at any time
- Email changes require re-verification (confirmation email to the new address)
- Date of birth can only be set once (cannot be modified after initial entry to prevent age verification bypass)
- All profile changes are logged in AuditLog

## Privacy and Compliance

### Data Subject Rights (GDPR)

**Right to Access (Article 15)**
- Customers can request a full export of their data via Settings > Privacy > Download My Data
- Export includes: profile, orders, reviews, addresses, payment methods (last four only), session history, notification history
- Export is generated as a ZIP file containing JSON and CSV files
- Processing time: up to 30 days (usually completed within 48 hours)
- All data access requests are logged in AuditLog

**Right to Rectification (Article 16)**
- Customers can update their profile information at any time
- For data corrections in historical records (e.g., wrong name on an old order), customer must contact support

**Right to Erasure (Article 17)**
- Customers can delete their account via Settings > Account > Delete Account
- Deletion process:
  1. Customer confirms deletion (double confirmation required)
  2. Account status → DELETED, deleted_at timestamp set
  3. All active sessions terminated immediately
  4. All saved payment methods revoked with the gateway
  5. Within 30 days: all PII fields nullified (email → NULL, name → NULL, phone → NULL, etc.)
  6. Non-PII data retained for financial reporting (order amounts, status, timestamps)
  7. Wishlists and cart data permanently deleted
  8. Review display_name changed to "Deleted User"
- Cannot be undone after PII nullification

**Right to Data Portability (Article 20)**
- Same export mechanism as Right to Access
- Data provided in machine-readable format (JSON)

**Right to Object (Article 21)**
- Customers can opt out of marketing via: (1) profile settings, (2) email unsubscribe link, (3) contacting support
- Marketing consent is tracked with a timestamp in the consent log
- Opting out does not affect transactional notifications (order confirmations, shipping updates)

### CCPA Compliance
- "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" link in footer
- ShopStream does not sell customer data to third parties
- Annual CCPA disclosure report published on the privacy page

### Cookie Consent
- First-party cookies: session management, cart persistence (essential, no consent needed)
- Analytics cookies: Google Analytics, Mixpanel (consent required in EU/UK)
- Marketing cookies: retargeting pixels (consent required everywhere)
- Cookie consent banner managed by OneTrust integration

## Customer Service Data Access

### Access Levels
| Role | Access |
|------|--------|
| L1 Agent | View customer profile (name, email, status), view order history, view shipment tracking |
| L2 Agent | L1 + view payment details (last four, status), initiate refunds up to $100, process returns |
| L3 Agent (Lead) | L2 + initiate refunds up to $500, merge customer accounts, override return policies |
| Manager | Full access, bulk data export, account suspension/deletion |

### Access Controls
- Customer service tools use role-based access control (RBAC)
- All data views are logged in AuditLog with actor_type: ADMIN
- Bulk operations (affecting >10 customers) require manager approval
- PII data is partially masked in the CS tool by default (email: j***@example.com, phone: ***-***-1234)
- Agents can unmask data when needed, which triggers an additional audit log entry

## Customer Analytics

### Key Metrics
- **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**: Predicted total revenue over the customer relationship
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Marketing spend / new customers acquired
- **Retention Rate**: Percentage of customers who make a repeat purchase within 12 months
- **Churn Rate**: Percentage of active customers who become inactive (no order in 12 months)
- **Net Promoter Score (NPS)**: Based on post-purchase surveys

### Segmentation
Customer segments are maintained in the analytics warehouse for marketing targeting:
- **New**: Registered within the last 30 days
- **Active**: At least one order in the last 90 days
- **At Risk**: Previously active but no order in 91-180 days
- **Dormant**: No order in 181-365 days
- **Churned**: No order in >365 days
- **VIP**: Platinum loyalty tier or CLV > $5,000

### Anonymized Analytics
- Customer-level analytics in the data warehouse use customer_id (pseudonymous)
- Aggregate analytics (cohort analysis, segment size) are fully anonymized
- Machine learning models for recommendation and personalization use hashed identifiers
- Analytics data is retained for 5 years; older data is aggregated to cohort level

## Data Quality Rules

### Validation at Registration
- Email: RFC 5322 compliant, max 255 characters, unique among active accounts
- Phone: E.164 format when provided
- Name: 1-100 characters per field, no HTML tags
- Date of birth: Must be at least 13 years ago (COPPA compliance)

### Ongoing Data Quality
- Email deliverability: Bounced emails are flagged after 3 consecutive bounces
- Phone validity: Invalid numbers (disconnected) are flagged during SMS delivery attempts
- Address verification: Addresses are re-verified with SmartyStreets when used for checkout
- Duplicate detection: Weekly batch job scans for potential duplicate accounts

### Data Quality Monitoring
- Dashboard tracks: duplicate rate, email bounce rate, address verification failure rate
- Alerts trigger when email bounce rate exceeds 2% (target: <1%)
- Monthly data quality report shared with the customer experience team
